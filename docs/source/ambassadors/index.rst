PsychoPy Ambassador Scheme
==========================

Our **PsychoPy Ambassadors** are enthusiastic members of the community who help spread the word about PsychoPy, provide local expertise, and contribute to the growth of open science.  
They act as connectors between the PsychoPy team and local researchers, educators, and students — helping to share knowledge, encourage adoption, and provide feedback to improve the software.

Whether through running workshops, translating documentation, mentoring new users, or advocating for open-source tools in research, our ambassadors are key to making PsychoPy accessible to everyone.

Want to become a PsychoPy Ambassador?
--------------------------------------
If you're passionate about open science and keen to help others discover PsychoPy, we’d love to hear from you!  
You can apply to join our scheme by filling in the application form here:  
`PsychoPy Ambassador Application Form <https://forms.clickup.com/4570406/f/4bf96-12812/PGCRQPBBHKHXPLP6JY>`_.

Please note: unfortunately, we can’t make everyone an ambassador — we want to be sure we can provide a good level of support to our existing ambassadors, so we accept new members only when we have the capacity to do so.

Benefits of being an ambassador:
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
- Recognition on our website and in community events
- Early access to new features and updates
- Opportunities to present at conferences or workshops
- Networking with other ambassadors and the PsychoPy core team
